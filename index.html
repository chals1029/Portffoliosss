04 — Process

Design Process

My approach to solving design challenges follows a structured yet flexible methodology.

1

Discover

Understand the user problem through research, interviews, and competitive analysis.

2

Ideate

Brainstorm solutions, sketch concepts, and map user flows based on insights gathered.

3

Wireframe

Create low and mid-fidelity wireframes to validate structure and layout decisions.

4

Deliver

Build high-fidelity prototypes, conduct usability tests, and iterate to final interface.
